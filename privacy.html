Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Welcome to S.H.E.L.L.Y. (Severe Hydrology & Environmental Live Logging Yearbook) / WeatherNow. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines exactly what information we collect, how it is used, and which third parties receive data.

1. Information We Collect

When you use this application, we collect or process the following information:

  • Location Data: To provide accurate weather forecasts, radar imagery, and severe weather alerts customized to your area, we require either your device's geographical coordinates (GPS) or a manually entered City/State/ZIP Code.
  • Application Settings: Your customized settings, such as selected themes, unit preferences, cycle speed, and alert configurations.

2. Data Storage

We believe your data belongs to you. We handle storage in the following manner:

  • Local Storage: All application settings, preferences, and your most recent location choice are stored locally on your device using your browser's (or app's) LocalStorage functionality. These preferences do not leave your device and are not uploaded to any remote server owned by us.
  • No Accounts: We do not require account registration, and we do not maintain a remote database of user accounts, identifying information, or personal preferences.

3. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

Because S.H.E.L.L.Y. is a live data dashboard, your geographic coordinates and search terms must be transmitted to external weather providers to retrieve live meteorological data. When the app fetches data, the following third parties may receive your location data:

  • Open-Meteo API (api.open-meteo.com & air-quality-api.open-meteo.com): Used to fetch all current conditions, hourly/extended forecasts, almanac information, and air quality metrics. Your exact geographic coordinates are sent to Open-Meteo with each data refresh. You can review their privacy policy at open-meteo.com.
  • National Weather Service (NWS) API (api.weather.gov): Used to pull active severe weather alerts for your area. The application transmits your coordinates to secure the correct county/zone alert codes.
  • Cloudflare / Cloudflare Workers: Used primarily as an API proxy for certain operations (such as server-side music playlists, announcements, or NWS radar imagery). Requests pass through Cloudflare infrastructure which acts as our network gateway.
  • Leaflet & Map Tile Providers: Radar maps load map frameworks and map imagery. IP addresses might be logged by the Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) corresponding directly to the map scripts used (e.g., Unpkg CDN) and the public domains from which radar frames or base maps are downloaded or proxied.

S.H.E.L.L.Y. does not sell your personal data or your location data to advertisers. Information shared with the API providers listed above is strictly used for fulfilling weather functionality.

4. Device Capabilities

This application requests the use of specific device hardware and capabilities:

  • Location / GPS: Requested strictly when you click the "Use my location" button for auto-detecting your coordinates.
  • Audio / Media: Utilized to play weather alerts via Text-To-Speech (TTS) and to index and stream local music files loaded strictly by your command. We do not transmit your local file structure to remote servers.

5. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. If the app begins interacting with additional third-party data services, this document will be updated to reflect how data is handled.
